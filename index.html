About Me

My name is Awolokun Oluwapelumi Favour, a young woman in her late 20s. I am an incredible young lady who dispise idleness. This young passionate woman started as an event hostess with an ushering agency called EVENT_BY JNIFTY in the year 2015. After 1 year of experience, i started working with other ushering agencies such as THE CYBLIS COMPANY, LILIAN K's EVENT, ONDO BEAUTIFUL AND ESPERIENCE USHERS, WALTERs EVENT and lots more. I graduated from Adeyemi Federal University of Education afflt to OAU in Ondo state where i obtained a degreee in Guidance and Counselling in the year 2022.

I started my own catering services in the year 2017 while still working as an event hostess, In 2023 i decided to make it official by going to a culinary school where i obtained a certificate of merit in catering, and pasteries. I started cooking for events, doing home services, birthday parties and get togethers.

I am also an aspiring software engineer with a passion for creating modern and accessible software solutions. In addition to my technical skills, I have a background as a chef, which has taught me discipline, creativity, and problem-solving in dynamic environments.
